Baffling Defenses

Target creature’s base power perpetually becomes 0.

Davriel, Soul Broker

+1: Until your next turn, whenever an opponent attacks you and/or planeswalkers you control, they discard a card. If they can’t, they sacrifice an attacking creature.

−2: Accept one of Davriel’s offers, then accept one of Davriel’s conditions.

−3: Target creature perpetually gets -3/-3.

Davriel's Withering

Target creature perpetually gets -1/-2.

Ethereal Grasp

Tap target creature. That creature perpetually gains „This creature doesn’t untap during your untap step” and „: Untap this creature.”

Faceless Agent

Zmiennokształtny

When Faceless Agent enters the battlefield, seek a creature card of the most prevalent creature type in your library.

Lumbering Lightshield

When Lumbering Lightshield enters the battelfield, target opponent reveals a nonland card at random from their hand. It perpetually gains „This spell costs more to cast.”

Manor Guardian

When Manor Guardian dies, each player seeks a nonland card with mana value 2 or less.

Plaguecrafter's Familiar

Dotyk śmierci

When Plaguecrafter’s Familiar enters the battlefield, choose a creature card in your hand. It perpetually gains deathtouch.

Ranger-Captain of Eos

Kiedy ten stwór wejdzie na pole bitwy, możesz wyszukać w twojej bibliotece kartę stwora o skrócie many 1 lub mniejszym, ujawnij ją i umieść w twojej ręce, po czym przetasuj.

Poświęć tego stwora: Twoi przeciwnicy nie mogą w tej turze rzucać nie-stworowych czarów.
